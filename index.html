How it works

Swap through Hyperliquid USA in three steps

Hyperliquid USA keeps the flow direct: connect a wallet, choose the asset pair, review the live route, then approve only when the app details match your intent.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app with a self-custody wallet. You keep control of your keys and approve actions from your own wallet.

2

Pick the swap

Select the asset you want to send, the asset you want back, and the route the app makes available for that pair.

3

Review and confirm

Check the quote, route, expected timing, and network costs in the app, then sign from your wallet when you are ready.

FAQ

Hyperliquid USA questions

01What is Hyperliquid USA?

Hyperliquid USA is a non-custodial DeFi swap page for users researching Hyperliquid access, HYPE swaps, wallet flow, and route details from a U.S. perspective.

02Is Hyperliquid USA non-custodial?

Yes. The app is designed around wallet connection and user-approved transactions. You review the route and sign from your own wallet instead of depositing into a custodial account.

03Which chains and assets are supported?

The page focuses on Hyperliquid, HyperEVM, Ethereum, HYPE, ETH, USDC, and app-listed assets. See the current list on the supported chains page.

04How long does a swap take?

Swap timing depends on wallet confirmation, route availability, network conditions, and the asset pair. The app shows the live route status before you confirm.

05What does it cost?

Costs can include network fees, route costs, and any price movement shown by the live quote. Hyperliquid USA does not invent static fees; the app displays the current cost before signing.
